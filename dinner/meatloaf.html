Meatloaf

Sam Sebastian

Meatloaf | Sam Sebastian | Prep: 10min | Cook: 55min

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350F
  2. Mix until well combined
    • Beef
    • Bread crumbs
    • onion
    • milk
    • beaten egg
    • ketchup (2 table)
    • Worcestershire sauce Until well combined
  3. Add meat mix into loaf pan
  4. Pat meat until even
  5. Combine
    • Ketchup (1/4 cup)
    • brown sugar
    • vinegar
  6. Pour sauce on top of meatloaf
  7. Spread into even layer
  8. Bake for 55min
  9. Let rest for 8-10 minutes before serving

Ingredients

Ingredients Amount Notes
Ground Beef 1 lbs 90%
Bread Crumbs 1 cup
Onion 1/2 cup yellow
Milk 1/2 cup
Egg 1
Ketchup 2 table 1/4 cup
Worcestershire Sauce 1 table
Parsley 1 tea
Salt 3/4 tea
Garlic Powder 1/2 tea
Black Pepper 1/4 tea
Brown Sugar 2 table
Red Wine Vinegar 1 table
