Classic Hamburgers

Sam Sebastian

Classic Hamburgers | Sam Sebastian | Prep: 20min | Cook: 10min

Directions

  1. Mix
    • Beef
    • Onion
    • Worcestershire
  2. Shape meat in 4 patties 3/4in thick
  3. Refrigerate 20 minutes
  4. Preheat Grill
  5. Season outside of burgers with Salt and Pepper
  6. Cook 5 minutes per side
  7. Let cool 5 minutes

Ingredients

Ingredients Amount Notes
Ground Chuck 1 lbs
Onion 1 table chopped
Worcestershire 2 Dash
Salt to taste
Pepper to taste
Rolls 4 x
