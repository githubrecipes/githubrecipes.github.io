Sloppy Joes

Sam Sebastian

Sloppy Joes | Sam Sebastian | Prep: 25min | Cook: 20min

Directions

  1. Mash
    • Beef
    • Baking soda
  2. Let sit 10-15 min to tenderize
  3. Preheat oven to 350F
  4. Heat
    • Butter Until Bubbling
  5. Add
    • Onion
    • Bell Pepper
    • Garlic
  6. Cook (while stirring) for 4-6 min
  7. Add
    • Beef
    • Salt
    • Pepper
    • Paprika
    • Cumin
    • Oregano
    • Mustard
  8. Cook Until mostly brown (~3 min)
  9. Add
    • Tomato Sauce
    • Tomato Paste
    • Worcestershire Sauce
    • hot sauce (to taste)
  10. Reduce heat
  11. Simmer for 10 min Until sauce has thickened

Ingredients

Ingredients Amount Notes
Ground Beef 1 1/2 lbs
Baking Soda 1/2 tea
Butter 2 table
Onion 1 x medium, finely diced
Red Bell Pepper 1 x medium, finely diced
Garlic 3 tea
Salt 1 tea
Pepper 1/2 tea
Paprika 1 1/2 tea
Cumin 3/4 tea
Oregano 1/2 tea
Dry mustard 3/4 tea
Tomato Sauce 14 oz
Tomato Paste 2 table
Worcestershire Sauce 1 table
Hot Sauce 1 tea
