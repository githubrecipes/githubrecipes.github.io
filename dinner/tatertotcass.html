Tater Tot Casserole

Sam Sebastian

Tater Tot Casserole | Sam Sebastian | Prep: 25min | Cook: 1hr

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350F
  2. Cook in pot
    • Sausage
    • Ground beef
    • Onion Until no longer pink Drain Return to pot
  3. Add
    • Soup
    • Beans
    • Corn
    • 1/2 of the Cheese
    • Milk
    • Garlic Powder
    • Salt
    • Cayenne Pepper
  4. Transfer to appropriately sized container
  5. Top with
    • Tater Tots
    • Rest of the Cheese
  6. Cover with aluminum foil
  7. Cook for 50 minutes
  8. Uncover
  9. Cook for 5-10 more minutes Or until bubbly
