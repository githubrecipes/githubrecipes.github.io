Vegetarian Buffalo Dip

Sam Sebastian

Vegetarian Buffalo Dip | Sam Sebastian | Prep: 10min | Cook: 1.5hr

Directions

  1. Combine in bowl
    • Sour Cream
    • Cream Cheese
    • Ranch Dressing Mix Until smooth
  2. Stir in
    • Cheese
    • Black beans
    • Mushrooms
    • Buffalo Wing Sauce
  3. Transfer to crockpot
  4. Cook for 1.5hr
  5. Serve with lime tortilla chips

Ingredients

Ingredients Amount Notes
Sour Cream 1 cup
Cream Cheese 8 oz
Ranch Dressing mix 1 x
Cheddar Cheese 2 cup
Black beans 1 can rinse, drain
Mushrooms 8 oz chopped
Buffalo Wing Sauce 1 cup
Tortilla Chips 1 bag lime!
