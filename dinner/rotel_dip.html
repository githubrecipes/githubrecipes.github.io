Rotel Dip

Sam

Rotel Dip | Sam | Prep: 15min | Cook: 15min

Directions

  1. Add oil to medium skillet over medium heat
  2. Add Sausage to skillet
    1. Cook until browned
    2. Break apart sausage as you cook.
  3. Drain Grease and return skillet to stovetop over medium-heat
  4. Add cream cheese and Tomatoes to skillet
  5. Stir frequently until cream cheese is completely melted and mixed
  6. Serve warm

Ingredients

Ingredients Amount Notes
Olive Oil 1 tea
Bulk Sausage 1 lbs
Diced Tomatoes 1 can Canned, w/green chilies
Cream Cheese 16 oz
Chips 1 bag
