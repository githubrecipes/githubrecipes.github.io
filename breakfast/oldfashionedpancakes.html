Old Fashioned Pancakes

Sam

Old Fashioned Pancakes | Sam | Prep: 5min | Cook: 15hr

Directions

  1. In large bowl sift:
    • Flour
    • Baking Powder
    • Salt
    • Sugar
  2. Combine milk, eggs and melted butter
    • Mix until smooth
  3. Oil and heat griddle or frying pan
  4. Pour ~1/4 cup batter on griddle
  5. Brown on both sides and serve

Ingredients

Ingredients Amount Notes
Flour 1 1/2 cup
Baking Powder 3 1/2 tea
Salt 1 tea
White Sugar 1 table
Milk 1 1/4 cup
egg 1 x
Butter 3 table melted
