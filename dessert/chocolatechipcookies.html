Chocolate Chip Cookies

Sam Sebastian

Chocolate Chip Cookies | Sam Sebastian | Prep: 20min | Cook: 10min each

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350F
  2. Cream
    • Butter
    • White Sugar
    • Brown Sugar Until smooth
  3. Beat in
    • eggs
    • vanilla
  4. Add
    • Baking Soda
    • Water
  5. Add
    • Flour
    • Cinnamon Recommended to do it 1 cup at a time
  6. Add
    • Chocolate Chips
  7. Place 1 1/2 in balls of batter onto trays
  8. Bake for 10 minutes Or until edges are lightly browned

Ingredients

Ingredients Amount Notes
Butter 1 cup
White Sugar 1 cup
Brown Sugar 1 cup
Eggs 2 x
Vanilla Extract 2 tea
Baking Soda 1 tea
Water 2 tea
Salt 1/2 tea
Flour 3 cups
Chocolate Chips 2 cups
Cinnamon 1 tea
