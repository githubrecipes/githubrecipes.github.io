Banana Bread

Sam Sebastian

Banana Bread | Sam Sebastian | Prep: 10min | Cook: 55min

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350F
  2. Mash
    • Ripe bananas
  3. Stir into bananas
    • Melted butter
  4. Mix
    • Baking soda
    • Salt
    • Sugar
    • Beaten egg
    • Vanilla extract
  5. Mix
    • Flour
  6. Pour batter into loaf pan
  7. Bake 50-60min Or until toothpick comes out clean
  8. Remove from oven and let cool in pan (3-5min)

Ingredients

Ingredients Amount Notes
Ripe Bananas 2-3 x 1 1/4-1 1/2 cups mashed
Butter 1/3 cup melted
Baking Soda 1 tea
Salt pinch
Sugar 1/2-1 cup sweetness range
Egg 1 x large, beaten
Vanilla Extract 1 tea
Flour 1 1/2 cup
