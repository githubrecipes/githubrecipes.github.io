Chicken Tenders

Sam

Chicken Tenders | Sam | Prep: 10min | Cook: 20min

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 400F
  2. Lightly grease baking tray
  3. Whisk in large bowl:
    • egg
    • oil
    • lemon juice
    • parsley
    • garlic
    • salt
    • pepper
  4. Coat chicken tenders in egg mixture
  5. Combine in small bowl:
    • Panko Breadcrumbs
    • Regular Breadcrumbs
    • Paprika
    • Garlic Powder
    • Onion Powder
    • Parmesan Cheese
  6. Coat egged chicken tenders in above mixture
  7. Arrange chicken tenders on greased baking tray
  8. Bake for 10 minutes
  9. Flip
  10. Bake for 5-10 minutes
  11. Enjoy!

Ingredients

Ingredients Amount Notes
Egg 1 x
Olive Oil 2 table
Lemon Juice 2 table
parsley 1 table
Minced Garlic 2 tea
Salt 3/4 tea
Black Pepper 1/4 tea
Chicken tenders 28 oz
Panko Breadcrumbs 1 cup
Regular Breadcrumbs 1/2 cup
Paprika 1 tea
Garlic Powder 1/2 tea
Onion Powder 1/2 tea
Parmesan Cheese 1/4 cup
