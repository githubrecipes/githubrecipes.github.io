Crockpot Shepard’s Pie

Sam Sebastian

Crockpot Shepard’s Pie | Sam Sebastian | Prep: 20min | Cook: 4-8hr

Directions

  1. Brown ground beef with onions in skillet
  2. Prep potatoes
    1. Boil potatoes until tender
    2. Drain
    3. Mash with 1 tablespoon of milk
  3. Mix
    • Beef mixture
    • Brown gravy mixes
    • garlic powder
    • onion powder
    • carrots
    • peas
    • cream of mushroom soup
  4. Spray Crockpot with cooking spray
  5. Pour beef mixture into Crockpot
  6. Spoon mashed potatoes on top
    • Spread evenly
  7. Cover and cook
    • high for 4 hours
    • low for 8 hours

Ingredients

Ingredients Amount Notes
Ground Beef 3 lbs
Onion 1/2 cup Chopped
Russet Potatoes 5x See recipe
Milk 1 tablespoon For potatoes
Brown Gravy Mix 2 oz 2-1oz envelopes
Garlic Powder 1 teaspoon
Onion Powder 1 teaspoon
Frozen carrots 12 oz Sliced
Frozen peas 12 oz
Cream of mushroom soup 10.5 oz
Paprika Sprinkling Optional
