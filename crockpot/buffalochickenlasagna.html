Buffalo Chicken Lasagna

Sam Sebastian

Buffalo Chicken Lasagna | Sam Sebastian | Prep: 25min | Cook: 4hr

Directions

  1. Cook chicken in olive oil Until no longer pink
  2. Drain
  3. Stir in
    • Wing Sauce
    • Spaghetti Sauce
  4. Mix in small bowl
    • Ricotta
    • Mozzarella Cheese
  5. Spread Sauce Mixture on bottom of crockpot
  6. Layer until depleted:
    • 3 noodles
    • 1 cup sauce
    • Peppers
    • cheese mix
  7. Cook on low for 4-5 hours

Ingredients

Ingredients Amount Notes
Ground Chicken 1 lbs
Olive Oil 1 table
Buffalo Wing Sauce 12 oz
Spaghetti Sauce 1 1/2 cups
Ricotta Cheese 15 oz
Mozzarella Cheese 2 cup
Lasagna Noodles 9 x
Sweet Red Peppers 2 x medium
