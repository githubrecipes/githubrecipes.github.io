Crockpot Irish Beef Stew

Irish beef stew1

Directions

  1. Combine and whisk
    • Salt
    • Pepper
    • Flour
  2. Toss beef cubes in mixture til well coated
  3. Melt butter in large pot
  4. IN batches
    • Brown meat on all sides
  5. Transfer browned beef and juices to slow cooker
  6. Cook onion and garlic in pot until fragrant
    • add tomato paste
      • cook 1 min
  7. Transfer mixture to slow cooker
  8. Add
    • Beer
    • Carrots
    • Potatoes
    • Beef broth
    • thyme
  9. Cook
    • high for 4 hours
    • low for 8 hours
    • Until beef is falling apart

Ingredients

Ingredients Amount Notes
Beef chuck roast 2lbs cut into 2in pieces
Salt 1/2 tea
Pepper 1/4 tea
Flour 2 table
Butter 2 table
Yellow onion 1x Large
Garlic 4x cloves
Tomato paste 3 table
Dark Irish Stout Beer 1x
Carrots 3x peeled and cut into 1in pieces
Potatoes 2x 1in pieces
Beef broth 3 cups
Thyme chopped
