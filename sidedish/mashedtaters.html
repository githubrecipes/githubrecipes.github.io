Mashed Potatoes

Sam Sebastian

Mashed Potatoes | Sam Sebastian | Prep: 10min | Cook: 20min

Directions

  1. Combine in large pot
    • Garlic
    • Potatoes
  2. Cover with water
  3. Bring to a boil
  4. Boil for 15-20 minutes Or until potatoes are tender enough to mash
  5. Drain
  6. Mash together
    • Milk
    • Butter
    • Potatoes
    • Parmesan Cheese
  7. Season to taste

Ingredients

Ingredients Amount Notes
Potatoes 8x chopped, optionally peel
Garlic 3 tea
Butter 2 table
Milk 1/2 cup
Salt 1 tea
Pepper 1 tea
Parmesan Cheese 1/3 cup
