Cheesey Broccoli

Sam

Cheesey Broccoli | Sam | Prep: 5min | Cook: 10min

Directions

  1. Wash broccoli
  2. Place in non-stick skillet with water
  3. Simmer over medium heat
  4. Cover and steam broccoli for 3-5 minutes
  5. Remove and place in bowl and cover
  6. Whisk
    • Milk
    • Cornstarch
  7. Pour into skillet and whisk over medium heat
    • until it thickens
  8. Set heat to low
  9. Add cheese
  10. Whisk until smooth
  11. Season to taste with salt and pepper
  12. Cover broccoli with cheese and serve.

Ingredients

Ingredients Amount Notes
Broccoli 4 cup
Water 1 cup
Milk 1 cup
Corn starch 1 table
Cheddar Cheese 1 cup
Parmesan 1 table
Salt to taste
Pepper to taste
